Project Context & The A* Algorithm

The Intelligent Emergency Exit Planner is designed to showcase how Artificial Intelligence pathfinding can be critically applied in dynamic disaster scenarios.

Why We Built This

During a fast-spreading indoor hazard (like a building fire), human visibility and cognitive decision-making are severely impaired. Autonomous rescue units—such as robot quadrupeds or tactical drones—can navigate these environments using robust mathematical pathfinding, predicting active fire zones to map out the safest evacuation routes or locate trapped survivors.

How The A* (A-Star) Algorithm Works

The A* algorithm guarantees the shortest mathematically possible path. It calculates physical routes using two factors: G-Cost (how far the drone has traveled from the start) and H-Cost (a heuristic estimate of how far the drone is from the exit).

In this simulation, we artificially inflate the cost of nodes adjacent to spreading fires. This forces the AI drone to aggressively seek safer detours around the hazard, even if it requires traversing a much longer physical distance, ensuring survival over speed.
